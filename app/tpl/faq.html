How do dragon genetics works?

Generally speaking... it's complicated. There are 11 interbreedable dragon races, proponent and recessive genes, genome mutations, gene penalty for incest and many more. For details please refer to our whitepaper and smart contract.

How fights will be held?

Fight will be a series of attack/defense moves by dragons which will be computed on-chain based on skills of each dragon. Then the fight will be played as an animation to participants and anyone interested in a results.
